Instructions

This is designed to check out your memory. It is cool to not have a photographic memory but it is still fun to try it out. It is like the concentration game that we all played. There are 3 levels, an easy level, a medium level & a hard level. For each level, you will be shown a before image with a bunch of objects. You will have 10 secs to look at it. Then, you will be presented with an after image and you have to figure out the difference. For the 1st level, there are 3 changes, for the medium & hard levels there is only 1 change.Take your time to figure it out!
